## Featured Projects

**[Air Quality Forecasting — India (PM2.5)](https://github.com/shaheeeeeeeeem/air-quality-forecasting)**
Daily PM2.5 forecasting across 5 Indian cities. LightGBM beat Prophet and persistence baselines by 31–57% on walk-forward CV (39 expanding-window folds/city), then was validated against genuinely unseen OpenAQ station data collected after training ended — 3 of 5 cities matched or beat their cross-validated error on that real-world holdout.
`Python` `LightGBM` `Prophet` `pandas` `scikit-learn` `Streamlit`
[Live demo](https://air-quality-forecasting-b33yzgtm36iyumuzwesxzj.streamlit.app/) · [Repository](https://github.com/shaheeeeeeeeem/air-quality-forecasting)

**[GNN-Based HIV Inhibition Prediction](https://github.com/shaheeeeeeeeem/molecular-property-prediction-gnn)**
GIN vs. GAT trained from scratch and compared head-to-head on OGB's `ogbg-molhiv` benchmark (GIN: 0.760 ROC-AUC, close to OGB's GCN baseline of 0.761). Extended to the harder 128-task `ogbg-molpcba` multi-label benchmark using a masked multi-task loss.
`PyTorch` `PyTorch Geometric` `OGB` `RDKit` `Streamlit`
[Live demo](https://molecular-property-prediction-gnngit-kctmdvzbrbrhddr7bj4hr5.streamlit.app/) · [Repository](https://github.com/shaheeeeeeeeem/molecular-property-prediction-gnn)

**[Code Comment Generation](https://github.com/shaheeeeeeeeem/code-comment-generation)**
A from-scratch Transformer (19M params) vs. fine-tuned BART on CodeSearchNet Python, compared on identical held-out examples. BART reached a higher ROUGE-L (0.245 vs. 0.219) using a fifth of the training data and a twentieth of the epochs — the expected effect of pretraining, reported directly rather than hidden.
`Python` `PyTorch` `Hugging Face Transformers` `BART` `ROUGE`
[Repository](https://github.com/shaheeeeeeeeem/code-comment-generation)
